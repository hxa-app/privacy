Privacy Policy

App: Crop Image & Photo Resize
Package name: com.hx.resize_image
Effective date: April 27, 2026

Your files stay on your device. Crop Image & Photo Resize processes selected images and cropped results on your device. We do not upload your selected images or cropped results to our servers or to the internet.

1. Overview

Crop Image & Photo Resize is a focused tool for cropping one image with preset ratios, custom crop, zoom, and position controls. This Privacy Policy explains what information may be processed when you use the app.

The app does not require you to create an account.

2. Local File Processing

When you select an image, the app uses that file only to crop and export the result you request.

Your selected image and cropped result are processed on your device. We do not upload your selected image or cropped result to our servers.

If you choose to share a result, the file is passed to the app or service that you select through the Android sharing system.

3. Information We May Collect

The app may collect or process the following types of information through third-party services:

  • App usage events, such as image selection, crop start, crop completion, save actions, share actions, and screen views.
  • Crash logs, diagnostics, device information, and app version information used to fix bugs and improve stability.
  • Advertising identifiers and ad interaction data used to provide and measure ads.
  • App configuration data used to control features such as ads, prompts, and app behavior.

We do not use this information to identify the content of your selected images.

4. Third-Party Services

Crop Image & Photo Resize may use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob for advertising.
  • Firebase Analytics for app usage analytics.
  • Firebase Crashlytics for crash reporting and diagnostics.
  • Firebase Remote Config for app configuration.

These third-party services may process information according to their own privacy policies. You can learn more about Google's privacy practices at https://policies.google.com/privacy.

5. Permissions

The app may request permissions depending on your Android version and the feature you use:

  • Photos, media, or file access: to select images and save cropped results.
  • Internet access: to load ads, analytics, crash reporting, and remote configuration.

6. Advertising

Crop Image & Photo Resize is supported by ads. Ads may be personalized or non-personalized depending on your region, device settings, consent choices, and Google policies. AdMob may use advertising identifiers and related data to provide, limit, and measure ads.

7. Data Retention

Generated files and task cache may be stored locally on your device so that you can save, share, or manage results. You can clear app cache through Android system settings.

Analytics, advertising, and crash diagnostic data are retained by the relevant third-party services according to their policies.

8. Children

Crop Image & Photo Resize is not intended for children under the age required by applicable law. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
